Our Story

Founded with a love for quiet mornings and warm cups, Sakura Café blends Japanese flavors with local ingredients. We whisk ceremonial-grade matcha, shape onigiri by hand, and keep our space calm and minimal.

We believe food should feel like care. That’s why we source responsibly, minimize waste, and craft everything to order.

  • Compostable packaging
  • Oat, soy, almond, and dairy options
  • Community nights & language exchange
